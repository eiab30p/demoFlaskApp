WTE is What the Eddy?

What The Eddy (WTE) is at the most simplest form is another blog. The idea came when helping fellow classmates in a class. Then again when I started to document a lot of different technologies at work. The overall consensus for both school and work is that I knew how to break it down in a simplistic terminology and with plenty of examples and list of resources used. The most important reason why I started the blog was that I think new developers or people looking to program need to know the obvious basics that are assume once you start but are not known when starting. I also enjoy teaching and helping others learn new things. Hopefully, WTE can show you some tips tricks and even something new to create the next revolutionary technology.
